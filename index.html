About Me

What sets me apart from everyone else at my level is my ability to employ active listening skills in combination with critical thinking and technical skills to not only find a solution that meets my clients' needs, but also allows me to anticipate needs they may not even know they have yet. I believe the key components of customer service include not only listening to the customer, but knowing what questions to ask in order to dig a little deeper. My main focus is doing everything in my power to help my customers achieve success in their business.

Every company with a presence on the internet should be concerned with protecting their assets. This includes proprietary data, customer data as well as infrastructure. How can you truly know your data and systems are safe if you never test them? That's what a security analyst does. I scan your network looking for vulnerabilities and report them back to you so that you can take measures to not only secure your network, but so you can sleep well at night knowing you will not receive any 2am phone calls that the reputation of your company is down the drain due to customer data leaks.

I am confident that my unique combination of hard and soft skills is not only rare to find in anyone else, but it also extremely valuable to have as a member of your team. I am focused, driven and determined to stay abreast of current events and changing technologies. My day does not end at the end of regular business hours.
